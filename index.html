Last Updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Calendar App collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

Calendar App is designed to help users view dates, days, months, and years in a simple calendar format. The app allows users to navigate between months and years and view the current date.

The app is built for simple calendar viewing purposes only and does not require login, registration, or personal profile creation.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from users.

The app does not ask for or collect:

  • Payment information
  • Login credentials

Calendar App only displays calendar dates, days, months, and years. The app does not access your personal calendar events from your device, Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or any other calendar account.

The app does not read, create, edit, delete, upload, or share personal calendar events.

4. App Permissions

Calendar App does not require sensitive device permissions to work.

The app does not request access to:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Location
  • Contacts

  • Call logs
  • Personal calendar accounts
